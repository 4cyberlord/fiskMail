# fiskMail — Future Improvements

Ideas and roadmap items to enhance the platform for students, admins, and the broader Fisk community. Use this as a reference when planning later releases.

---

## Package & mail handling

- **QR code scanning for packages** — Scan a QR code on incoming mail or parcels to auto-fill recipient (e.g. by student ID or barcode), speeding up receive-mail and reducing manual entry and errors. Admins could also scan at pickup to mark items as picked up instantly.
- **Barcode / label printing** — Generate and print labels or barcodes when receiving mail so each item is uniquely identifiable and scannable at the desk.
- **Batch receive** — Accept multiple items in one flow (e.g. scan several packages in sequence) for high-volume days.

---

## Mobile experience

- **Mobile app (students)** — Native or PWA so students can check notifications, see “you have mail,” and view mail items on the go. Push notifications for new mail and reminders.
- **Mobile app (admins)** — Receive-mail and pickup-desk workflows optimized for tablets or phones (e.g. scan at the desk, quick lookup, mark picked up) for staff who are not at a desktop.
- **Responsive and touch-friendly** — Ensure all current flows work well on small screens and touch (already partially there; continue refining).

---

## Users (students)

- **Notification preferences** — Let students choose channels (in-app, email, and later SMS/push) and frequency (e.g. immediate vs digest).
- **Mail history** — Simple history of past pickups and notifications for reference.
- **Calendar / deadlines** — Optional tie-in to “pick up by” or “mailroom hours” so students see reminders in a calendar view.

---

## Admins

- **Analytics and reporting** — Dashboards for volume (received/picked up), average time to pickup, and reminder effectiveness to improve operations.
- **Bulk actions** — Send reminders or notifications to multiple students at once (e.g. “all with unclaimed mail”).
- **Roles and permissions** — More granular roles (e.g. mailroom-only vs full admin) for larger teams.
- **Audit log** — Who received what, when it was picked up, and who performed the action for compliance and troubleshooting.

---

## Platform and scale

- **API for integrations** — Optional API so other campus systems (e.g. housing, enrollment) can trigger notifications or check mail status.
- **Multi-location mailrooms** — Support multiple pickup locations or desks with location-specific queues and hours.
- **Offline-capable flows** — Let admins record receives or pickups when connectivity is poor and sync when back online.

---

## Accessibility and inclusivity

- **Accessibility audit** — Formal a11y review and fixes (keyboard, screen reader, contrast) so the app is usable by everyone.
- **Language and localization** — Prepare for multiple languages if the community expands.
